Requirements Document

Problem Statement

Farmers often sell their crops at unfair or sub-optimal prices due
to lack of real-time market information and difficulty in comparing 
multiple buyer offers.

Target Users

- Small and medium farmers
- Crop buyers (wholesalers/retailers)

Functional Requirements

- Farmers can enter crop type, location, and expected price
- Farmers can view multiple buyer price offers
- System provides a fair price recommendation
- System highlights best buyer options
- System detects and warns about unfair prices
- System gives feedback on farmer’s expected price

Non-Functional Requirements

- Easy to use for non-technical users
- Fast response time
- Scalable for multiple users
- Clear and understandable output
